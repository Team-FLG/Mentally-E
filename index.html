About Us

Mentally is a mental consulting platform designed to help individuals optimize their mental well-being and reach their full potential. It provides personalized guidance from trained professionals, empowering tools and techniques, and continuous support, all accessible conveniently through a digital platform. Mentally prioritizes holistic well-being, offering confidential and tailored solutions to help individuals develop resilience, manage stress, and foster a positive mindset for a fulfilling life.

DISORDERS

Anxiety Disorders:

These disorders involve excessive worry, fear, or unease.

Examples :- generalized anxiety disorder (GAD), panic disorder, social anxiety disorder, and specific phobias.

Mood Disorders:

These disorders primarily affect a person's emotional state.

Examples :- Depressive disorder (depression), bipolar disorder, and persistent depressive disorder (dysthymia).

Eating Disorders:

These disorders involve unhealthy attitudes and behaviors related to food and body weight.

Examples :- Anorexia nervosa, bulimia nervosa, and binge-eating disorder.

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD):

PTSD can develop after experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event. It involves symptoms like flashbacks, nightmares, avoidance, and hyperarousal.

Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD):

ADHD is characterized by difficulties with attention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity, which can impact a person's daily functioning and relationships.

Psychotic Disorders:

These conditions involve a loss of contact with reality and may include symptoms like hallucinations, delusions, and disorganized thinking.

Examples :- Schizophrenia
